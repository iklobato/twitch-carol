Frequently asked

What every streamer asks before connecting.

Does this break Twitch's rules?
No. The connection is Twitch's official login (OAuth) with read-only permissions. It's the same mechanism other tools use. We never act on your channel.
Will it lag or mess with my stream?
No. Nothing runs on your PC or in OBS. Collection and analysis happen on our server, in parallel. You stream normally.
Do you sell or share my data?
No. Your data is yours, used only to build your dashboard. Access tokens are encrypted and you can disconnect whenever you want.
Do I need to be an affiliate or partner?
Not to start. Without affiliate you already see followers, audience, which topic engages and best times. The bits and subs numbers only show up after you monetize, because that's when Twitch releases that data.
How much does it cost?
During the beta, nothing. You get in free and even help decide what we build first.
Does it work for a small channel?
Yes, and that's where it helps most: with little margin for error, knowing which topic pulls chat and money makes a real difference in your next stream.
